Reseller Hosting Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

1. General Responsibility

1.1. The reseller is fully responsible for all accounts created under their reseller account.

1.2. Any violation by a reseller's client is treated as a violation by the reseller.

1.3. Ignorance of abuse by end-users is not a valid defense.

2. Disk Usage & Storage Policy

2.1. Reseller hosting is NOT backup or file storage.

2.2. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Video hosting or streaming
  • Storing backups (cPanel, WordPress, server dumps)
  • Large compressed files (.zip, .tar, .tar.gz) over 2GB
  • ISO files, software distributions, media archives

2.3. Any account exceeding fair usage may be suspended without notice.

3. Inode & File Limits

3.1. Each cPanel account must remain within assigned inode limits.

3.2. Accounts exceeding 90% inode usage are subject to suspension.

3.3. Common inode abuse includes:

  • Excessive cache files
  • Backup folders inside public_html
  • File hoarding

4. CPU, RAM & Process Abuse

4.1. Resellers must not run:

  • Crypto miners
  • Background daemons
  • Long-running processes
  • High-resource scripts without approval

4.2. Accounts causing server load issues will be throttled or suspended.

5. Email & Spam Policy

5.1. Sending unsolicited email (spam) is strictly forbidden.

5.2. Bulk mailing scripts require prior approval.

5.3. Accounts causing IP blacklisting will be immediately suspended.

6. Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Phishing, scam, or fraudulent sites
  • Malware, spyware, or exploit kits
  • Adult content involving minors
  • Warez, cracked software, pirated content

7. Overselling Rules

7.1. Resellers may not advertise "Unlimited Disk" or "Unlimited Resources".

7.2. Misrepresentation of server resources is grounds for suspension.

8. Suspension & Termination

8.1. The provider reserves the right to:

  • Suspend individual cPanel accounts
  • Suspend the reseller account
  • Terminate service without refund for severe abuse

8.2. Data recovery after termination is not guaranteed.

9. Backups Disclaimer

9.1. Backups are provided as a courtesy, not a guarantee.

9.2. Resellers are responsible for maintaining independent backups.

10. Acceptance

By using the reseller service, the reseller agrees to:

  • This policy
  • Fair usage enforcement
  • Automated abuse detection
